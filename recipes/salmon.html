Garlic Butter Salmon

After trying many different salmon recipes, this is by far my favorite alongside fresh rice or pasta.

Ingredients

  • 200g salmon fillet
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 teaspoon garlic Powder
  • 1 Tablespoon olive oil

Steps

  1. Grab the salmon and remove the skin. Cut it into small pieces.
  2. Sprinkle salt over the salmon and let it marinate for 15 minutes.
  3. Heat a pan with olive oil and butter until melted, then add the salmon.
  4. Sprinkle garlic powder across each individual piece and cook until golden.
  5. Flip each piece, but be ready to remove them soon.
  6. Check to see if the other side is cooked, and remove quickly to prevent overcooking.
  7. Place it on a plate, and serve with rice or pasta. Enjoy!
