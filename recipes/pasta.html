Creamy Tomato Pasta

This creamy tomato pasta is easy to make and one of my favorite dishes I made myself.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Garlic
  • Tomato paste
  • Heavy Cream
  • Basil

Steps

  1. Heat up water in a pot.
  2. Separate the pasta and place all the ingredients used on the table, open.
  3. Put the pasta in the water and add salt.
  4. Heat a pan, add garlic, tomato paste, and heavy cream.
  5. Add salt and basil.
  6. Finish the sauce by adding butter and tasting to check if the salt is good.
  7. Remove the pasta from the water and drain it.
  8. Place the pasta in the pan with the sauce, add more butter and mix the pasta with the sauce.
  9. Serve on a plate. Enjoy!
